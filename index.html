My Dream Destination...

The Maldives are large archapeligo located in South Asia.

You should go to the Maldives because of it's sunny and fresh environemnt.
The water surrounding it is clear and you can see the animals up close from your resort.
The resort has a view of the beach making it a perfect vacation for you and your familiy.
The sound of waves that clash against the beach and makes it calming to the mind which is what you would want from a vacation.

The Maldives has a tourist attraction called "The Sea of Stars", at night the bioluminescent plankton around the ocean turn into a bright blue at night making an illusion of stars. The Sea of Stars at night are present around the shoreline. Due to this the Sea became a great tourist attraction and looks magical in person. The Maldives has many interesting tourist attractions allowing tourists to have many options to enjoy their time there.
